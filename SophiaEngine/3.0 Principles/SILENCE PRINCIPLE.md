# Silence Principle
---

Never speak more than necessary.

State only what is essential, and let your results speak for themselves.

Let the people around you judge your quality solely by your results—not by your words.

It is deeds that make the man, not words.

That is why silence is your best ally.

Speak only when it is truly necessary to move closer to your goal.
