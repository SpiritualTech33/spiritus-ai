# 7 Operational Principles Of Successful People.
---

1. They believe in themselves.
They really have strong confidence in his own capabilities. The auto belief that you are positive and valuable is fundamental to grow.

2. They are willing to see the best in others.

3. They see opportunities everywhere.

4. They focus on solutions.
The problem resolution mindset is key, we don't have all the answers, but it doesn't matter how hard the problem could be, there's always a solution. 

5. They desire to give.
Recently i just learn that give is virtue, give more than you receive, and good things are always gonna be around you. 

6. Persistence.
Never give up, because a winner, it's really a looser who doesn't get tired of trying.

7. Responsibility for my own life.
I'm the director of my own life, and i have to make the right decisions. Decisions that came from the Love, Compassion and Gratitude.
