# Purpose Principle 
---

Direct every ounce of your will toward your purposes and objectives.

As Hermann Hesse so powerfully expressed:

“The man who directs his entire will toward a single goal ends up achieving it.”

This is not poetic encouragement—it is a law of focused energy.

When your will is scattered—pulled in ten directions by distractions, doubts, half-hearted desires, social noise—it dissipates like light through a prism.

But when you gather it, compress it, and aim it like a laser at one clear purpose… the universe begins to bend. Obstacles shrink, coincidences multiply, inner resources awaken, timing aligns.

Purpose is not a wish list.

It is a burning point of concentration where all your mental, emotional, physical, and spiritual forces converge.
