# The Principle Of Polarity 
---

There are no absolute separations or hard boundaries between things—only gradients, degrees, shades along a continuous spectrum. All opposites are really the same thing viewed from different angles or positions on that scale.

Take good and evil. Where exactly does good end and evil begin?
Is there a precise moment when an action crosses from one into the other?
Or is it a sliding scale—selfless love at one extreme, pure malice at the other, and every human choice falling somewhere between, differing only in degree?

The same with heat and cold.
Where does heat stop and cold start?
There is no magic dividing line at, say, 15 °C where “warm” suddenly becomes “cold.”
It is the same phenomenon—molecular motion—measured from different reference points.
What we call “cold” is simply less heat; what we call “hot” is simply more heat.

The difference is degree, not kind.
