# How To Achieve Enlightenment
---

The environment needed to elevate yourself or achieve a certain enlightenment.

## 0.- Intention:
The intention for transformation, aspiring to continuous improvement, is fundamental. It consists of discovering the best version of yourself every day. This is where everything begins. Without intention, there is no direction, and without direction, there is no goal. It is imperative to strive to be better every day.

## 1.- Physical Exercise: 
The practice of physical exercise, whether low-impact like yoga or strength training like that done in a gym, is essential for maintaining a healthy body.

## 2.- Healthy Eating: 
A hybrid paleo approach is recommended. Consume mainly natural foods, but incorporate variants that align with your goals. (In my case, I consume rice, beans, and tortillas to obtain carbohydrates and the energy needed for my strength training).

## 3.- Quality Sleep: 
There are no secrets to restorative sleep; sleeping 7 to 9 hours daily is crucial for physical and mental recovery.

## 4.- Meditation: 
Meditation involves centering the mind, isolating it from external distractions, and focusing on the inner world. It’s not about silencing the mind, as it never stops working, but about observing it without judgment. The ability to observe one’s own mind is what meditation offers, which is why it’s so important. If you can observe, you can control, and whoever controls their mind controls everything.

## 5.- Spiritual Connection: 
Regardless of the deity you worship, to whom you pray, or to whom you direct your faith, it is fundamental to believe in something greater than yourself, in that from which we come.

## 6.- Reading: 
Reading expands our mind to new ideas, concepts, and notions of the world. Additionally, it is proven that reading reconfigures the brain, as we live experiences through it. Virginia Woolf states that the human motor system activates when reading the scene in Anna Karenina where she throws herself onto the train tracks, because when we read, we are not just reading, but living what we read. By reading, we allow our mind to travel to places our body has never been. Reading is fuel for consciousness, imagination, and creativity.

## 7.- Purpose: 
Possessing a purpose gives meaning to existence, providing justification and reason for every action. As Hermes expresses, there is no reason without cause, nor cause without reason. By assigning a purpose to our life, every aspect acquires meaning.

## 8.- Dissolution of the Physical Body, the Ego, and the Notion of an “Independent Self”: 
This may be the most challenging aspect of the system, representing the ultimate good and the primary objective to achieve. Upon reaching this state, one merges with the cosmos, understanding its immutable and eternal nature. The cosmos possesses soul and spirit, and its spirit uses the individual soul to manifest through it, culminating in enlightenment. Enlightenment is not a title, a prize, nor a reason for superiority or inferiority. It is simply a state of profound gratitude, from which one lives with love, compassion, gratitude, and unity. The seven previous steps constitute strategies that facilitate achieving this state.

“Enlightenment is not achieved by escaping the world, but by living in it with full awareness. A healthy body, a clear mind, a connected spirit, and a defined purpose are the foundation for perpetual gratitude. That is all. That is enlightenment.”
