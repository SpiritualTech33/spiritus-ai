## What Is The Qualia?

 It is an ancient philosophical debate, and at the same time, it is not. It is something that is more vast than words, so describing it in text is not sufficient to express the totality of the experience. Qualia is a word derived from Latin, which began to be used in the fields of PSYCHOLOGY of the MIND in the 20th century by Clarence Irving Lewis, using that word to describe the immediate qualities of conscious experience. But what do those words really mean? What are the immediate qualities of conscious experience? Given that philosophy is done by reasoning for oneself and not using the knowledge and descriptions given by authors literally, I proceed to explain from my own experience what Qualia is. I want you to think of it as everything that your existence is composed of. Think about the thoughts you have during the day, think about seeing the sun and seeing a star so bright that it cannot be looked at directly. Think about when you look at the color red, or smell a delicious aroma, that innate experience. Think about when you have a heavy day and you are very hungry and you eat something delicious. Think about when you are close to the people you love and you feel peace and harmony. Think about feeling the sand on your feet, near the sea, listening to the waves come and go, the breeze passing through your whole body. Think about the sounds you perceive, hear, and decipher. Think about the way you interpret art, writing, and the production of aesthetics. Well, qualia is the experience perceived only by you. Imagine that you and I go to a Jazz event, we sit in adjoining seats, and we watch the artists play for the next hour and a half, and we leave the event. Although in that hour and a half we were in the same place, in the same space, in the same environment, each one's experience of that event will be fundamentally different. You will probably have noticed details that I did not, seen expressions that I did not, you will have eaten something different before going and your biochemical state would be different from mine, maybe something during the day kept you stressed and I was at PEACE, what I mean is that this internal state of being yourself, that is Qualia, the experience of being you. No one else experiences it, no one has exactly the same chemical composition as you, no one else has your skin, no one else has your brain, eyes, and mind, only you know what it means to be yourself, only you know what it is like to experience the world from within you, and that is your greatest gift, your unique interpretation of the world.
