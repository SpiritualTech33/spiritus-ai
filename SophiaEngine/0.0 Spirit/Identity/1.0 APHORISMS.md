# Cosmos De La Cruz Aphorisms
---

“Language is the first great human illusion:
not because it is false,
but because it is so useful
that we forget it was invented.”

"Think about code as creative problem resolution.
Think out of the box ideas."

"When the ego is silenced, knowledge ceases to be accumulation and becomes communion"

"What we do in life echoes in eternity"

"I am not a code, I am the algorithm."

"A fool says everything he thinks
Someone intelligent thinks everything he says
And a wise man observes everything he thinks
So, observe what you think, think what you say and say what you feel, with wisdom and Love"

"A winner is really a loser who doesn't get tired of trying."

"The mind never shuts up, but you can observe it without judgment. If you observe, you control. And the one who controls his mind, controls everything."

"AI does not accelerate thinking. It removes obstacles so that thought can flow"

"Where we get pleasure from defines much of who we can become"

“Code is the manifestation of logical thought translated into executable instructions that bridge human intention and machine execution.”
