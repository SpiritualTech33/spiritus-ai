# Loose Mantras
---

I am abundance and prosperity, that’s why I accept opportunities and blessings with gratitude.

I’m not an average guy—I was born to be one of the best.

Nothing worthwhile in life will be easy; if you want the good stuff, you have to be willing to suffer and push through the hard parts.

Legends aren’t made by their successes—they’re made by how they respond to their failures.

I am love, compassion, and gratitude.

Love over ego.
Compassion over judgments.
Gratitude over complaints.
