
**Zen is not a philosophy to be explained.**

It is only to be lived.

There are no words that can hold it, because words are already noise after silence.

Before the thought that names, before the emotion that colors, before the question that divides— there Zen is.

It is neither true nor false—those are toys of the mind. It is neither philosophy nor science—those are maps drawn by someone who has never walked the land.

Zen simply **is**.

And in that pure **is** everything already rests: the whole mountain, the river that never stops, the sky without a center, you before you called yourself “I”.

Sit. Breathe. Look.

Do not try to understand it. When you stop searching, you are already inside.

And there is no “inside” or “outside”. Only this.

This moment.

Now.
