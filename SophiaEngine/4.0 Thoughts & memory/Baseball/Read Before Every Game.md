# Before Game Tips
---
Remember: hitting is not a science, it is not an algorithm, and it is not a battle.
You are not competing against the pitcher or the fielders. Because hitting is not about that.
Hitting is an art, a manifestation of the soul.
A moment of peace and harmony. Hitting is a martial art.

Hitting is not thought—it is felt.
Hitting is meditation in motion.

Remember: don’t overthink.
Step into the box comfortably—legs slightly bent, hands relaxed and close to your body—and attack the pitcher.

Keep it simple.
Think of the greats: Reggie Jackson, Willie Mays, Hank Aaron.
Tony Gwynn, Ken Griffey Jr., Barry Bonds.
Simple mechanics, yet extraordinarily effective.

See the ball.
Feel the rhythm.
Trust your hands.
Let your soul swing.

That’s it.
