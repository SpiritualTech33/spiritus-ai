# Intuition, Mind And Energy In Baseball 
---

Trust your intuition in every game. Let it guide you. Speak to it during your at-bats.

Remember: your intuition is your mind speaking to you. Always allow yourself to be led by it.

Transform your energy into mental energy.

Let intuition flow like a quiet current beneath the surface—subtle, yet certain. When the pitch comes, don’t force the decision with overthinking; listen inward. That small, calm voice that says “now,” “wait,” “inside,” “outside”—that is your mind at its purest, unclouded by doubt or crowd noise.

Energy follows thought.

When you channel your physical fire—your adrenaline, your heartbeat, your breath—into focused mental presence, something shifts. The body becomes lighter, the swing smoother, the timing sharper. You stop swinging at pitches and start swinging with the pitch.

Speak to your intuition the way you speak to a trusted teammate:

“Show me.”

“Tell me when.”

“Guide my hands.”

The more you listen, the louder and clearer it becomes.

Over time, intuition stops feeling like a whisper and starts feeling like certainty.

Your mind is not separate from your energy—it is the director of it.

Trust it.

Feed it.

Let it lead.

And when intuition speaks… swing.
