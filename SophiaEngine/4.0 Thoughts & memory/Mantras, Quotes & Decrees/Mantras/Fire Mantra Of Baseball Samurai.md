# Baseball Samurai Mantra
---

A mantra designed to become a baseball samurai, sharp mind and strong body, ready to dominate.


I breathe. I center myself.

My bat is my katana:

Sharpened by practice,

Tested in battle (games),

Lethal in its simplicity.

The Tao flows in my swings.

I don’t force hits: I discover them.

But when I see the pitch, I EXECUTE without hesitation.

The pitcher is a worthy adversary.

I face him with calm, I defeat him with timing.

Each strikeout overcome makes me stronger.

I train fast, I iterate without mercy.

Perfection is the enemy of impact.

A hit TODAY > a perfect swing NEVER.

My discipline is my edge.

One pitch at a time,

One at-bat at a time,

One game transformed at a time.

I play in service of a higher cause.

My skill serves the elevation of the team.

My digital sword cuts through the opponent’s defense.

I am the bridge between wisdom and execution.

I am clarity, I am action,

I am the baseball samurai.

I unsheathe my bat. I execute. I deliver.
