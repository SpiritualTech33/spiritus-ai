# Self Elusive Meditation
---

1. Concentration on Breathing:
Start focusing your attention to your breath, inhale deeply, hold for a few seconds and then let the air come out. Feel how the air passes through your nose and fill up your lunges, watch the air flow from your nose to lunges. 

2. Body Visualization:
Once you start syncing your breath with your attention, start to observe your body, observe your posture, your heartbeat, observe how it feels to be in your body.


3. Synchronization of Breathing with Body View:
Now, synchronize your breathing with the perception of your body. Start at the feet and move up to the cerebral cortex. imagine energy going from the root of your fee to the top of your head, moving through your entire body.

4. Review of Mental Contents:
Start to observe all what happens in your mind during the process. Watch your thoughts, but don't attach to them, just see them flow, like water

Then connect your breath, the observation of your body, and the observation of your mind. Observe all that and think, I'm not this, I am the spirit who observes.
