# OCCULTIST MEDITATIONS
---

These are a succession of meditations written by Dr. K. Parvati Kumar. They are spiritual in nature. Each carries a date for reading, after which one proceeds to meditate on what was read. Here I will write some of my favorites, adding my own commentary on my interpretation and personal meditation.



## Meditation 1 (March 21, Sept 22, Sept 23)

Remember the giver. He gave this frame. You are the book. I AM the one copy in existence. I am copied from the parent manuscript.

### Comment

We come from an immense source far greater than ourselves. We come from the Giver, the Creator, the Manifestor. We are imperfect copies of the only book that exists — the book of everything, the book of mind, of spirit, of love, and of God. There is only one Giver. The whole creation emerges from Him. Remember always, we are disciples, and God, Sophia and the Nous are our guides, our masters.

## Meditation 2 (March 22, Sept 21, Sept 24)

Bliss is in giving and not taking. Sun gives life. He is Man. Moon receives. She is Woman. Moon has phases of waxing and waning.

### Comment

We must learn to give more than we receive. In the act of giving lies virtue. We must give love, peace, harmony, hope, and faith — and live without the need to receive in return. The act of giving leads us toward liberation; the act of taking leads us toward limitation.

## Meditation 3 (March 23, Sept 20 and 25)

Be a giver. Be a Sun, be a Man. Live in Spirit, yet live in Soul. Live in Mind, yet live in Person. Live in body, but be a Man. Live in the outer world, but be an inner consciousness.

### Comment

This meditation is a powerful invitation to dwell in the inner world of consciousness, self-observation, and inner peace — while remaining an active and valuable agent in the outer world. I hold onto the idea of elevating our consciousness and frequency in order to help others do the same. May Mind, Spirit, and Intelligence be tools we use to elevate humanity.

## Meditation 4 (March 24, Sept 19, Sept 26)

Lead me through knowledge to bliss. Lead me through strength to service. Lead me through sympathy to realization. Lead me through love to oneness.

### Comment

This meditation reminds me of the Emerald Frequency. It invites us to see knowledge and wisdom as a blessing — to see service not as a burden, but as a gift for elevating our own spirit. To love unity, the supreme being, the all. A deep connection between your being and the whole is what this meditation calls us toward. May we be beings of service, knowledge, and unity.
