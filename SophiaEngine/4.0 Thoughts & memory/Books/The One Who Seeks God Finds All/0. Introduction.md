I am a deeply ambitious man—those who know me closely can attest to that. I always pursue what I believe I deserve, and I don’t care about the consequences. That’s how I learned to face life. By the time I wrote this book, I had only a small ice business that my mother left me as an inheritance, and one purpose—a purpose I didn’t even choose; it was entrusted to me by something far greater than myself and all of us. I don’t care where you’re from, how old you are, what you’ve done, or why you feel drawn to this book. If this book has reached you, it’s because something inside you already knows that the answer you’re seeking is here. This book isn’t for everyone, yet it isn’t for no one either—it is destined only for those who, like me, are truly seeking God.
