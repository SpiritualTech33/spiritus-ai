On the path to glory, to success, to the conquest of your deepest desires, stones, walls, thorns, and abysses await you. You will suffer, without a doubt, you will bleed, you will hurt yourself, you will feel that the limit has been reached. But, you know what? That is the precise moment to push with all your strength, to strive to the maximum, to fight with the passion of a lion. If greatness were easy, everyone would be champions, everyone would achieve immortality. The difference lies in the capacity to push, in the tenacity of the legendary, of those who are never forgotten. If you stop every time it hurts, every time you suffer, every time fatigue defeats you, are you really trying? Are you really preparing for battle? Limits were created to be broken, to be surpassed. When you feel that exhaustion consumes you, when the challenge seems insurmountable, go back to the origin, remember why you fight, why you train with fervor, why you surrender to combat. You are a gladiator, born to fight, to resist, to not break, to continue when others give up. If you truly wish to reach your dreams, if you truly yearn to reach the top that you visualize in your mind, there is no truce, no rest, no complaints, only work, sweat, and blood. Embrace the pain, make it yours, turn it into your ally, love it as God loves you, because it is pain that forges the strongest warriors. Don't stop until you achieve victory.

And every time you feel weak, every time tears threaten to fall, every time surrender stalks you, think of God, of the mission He has set out for you, think of Him and His infinite love, and remember that every drop of sweat, every training session, every effort you make is a prayer in devotion to His greatness. Let Him be your guide, surrender to His will, eliminate the ego and let your results speak for you.
