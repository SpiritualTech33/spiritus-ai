I do not fear darkness because in it I was created, we come from everything and into everything we become. I do not fear adversity because it has strengthened me. Nor do I fear failure because it has taught me. The great teachers of life always come wrapped in fear, doubts, discomfort, and pain. It is you who decides what to do with all that, those energies that are born from darkness can be the foundations of your greatest work or the dynamite that destroys your stability, the one who decides what it is, is you. You are a creator, you are a warrior who came to this world to prove to yourself how valuable you are. You are a gladiator, and every day you must fight to reach your highest version, seek yourself, every day, as if you don't know yourself.
