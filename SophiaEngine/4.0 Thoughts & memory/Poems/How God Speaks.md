God speaks through dreams at night, and through symbols and synchronicities during the day. You know it’s Him when you feel that current emerging from your back, flooding your entire body. You know it’s Him when tears well up in your eyes, because you feel His love and mercy. And you know it’s Him when that which you’ve fought so hard for arrives, with faith and grace.
